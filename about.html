About Us

Purpose Statement

Pitt County Animal Services is a local shelter that rehabilitates stray and abandoned animals. The main goal of the Pitt County Rescue Pets & Adoption site is to find rescue pets the best home possible.

Goals:

  • To gain customers
  • To get the site noticeable
  • To increase website traffic

Objectives:

  • Creating an online adoption process
  • Using social media for updates
  • Advertising through social media
