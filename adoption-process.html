Adoption Process

The steps of the adoption process consist of a:

  1. Questionnaire: Questions that pertain to your household, along with the type of pet you want to bring home.
  2. Basic interview: Face-to-face meeting about the questionnaire (follow-up).
  3. Meet-and-greet: Pet interaction with the potential owners.
  4. Contract: Finalized documents and paperwork of your new pet that outline the expectations of the shelter.

We hope you join in on the adopting fun!
